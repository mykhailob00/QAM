On this page I'm telling about my favorite musician - Will Wood.

Will Wood is a popular musician with over 1M listeners on his main profile on Spotify, thus he has another Spotify profile of his old group - Will Wood and Tapeworms with over 600k listeners. Will Wood started taking part in various bands and making own music somewhere in 2011, creating songs in a various mixtures of genres: Indie, folk, jazz, rock and roll, avant-garde, alternative rock, etc. According to his Spotify profile description, he owns three pet rats named "Snooter Man", "Mister Bubbles" and "Bastard". Will primarily perfomes in USA alone, making jokes and monologues in-between songs.
